Who We Are

We are Ryze, a one-stop shop for startups and SMEs by providing quality work at cost-efficient prices in less time.

We are building a pre-screened community of student freelancers who are willing to earn money and constantly grow their skills through regular supply of freelance projects. Hence making them financially independent and skilled by giving them relevant industry experience at the right time. The idea is to revolutionize the gig-economy by looking beyond just freelancing and tapping into segments like ed-tech and providing relevant courses at costs like no other.

It is the time to rise together, to rise with Ryze.
Folowing is the workflow at Ryze-

We understand your requirement

Our project managers will go through your project details and prepare a proposal.

The requirements are converted into tasks

Based upon the type and complexity of the requirement, we'll divide it into further tasks according to skills of the student freelancers.

Ryze trusted gig workers are chosen and alloted tasks

According to the skills of the students, they are alloted tasks from the company's requirement.

Execution is monitored realtime by Ryze and product is delivered to the company upon completion

We at Ryze ensures the product is delivered with best quality so every task is closely monitored by a skill expert and when the product is completed perfectly as per the requirement, it is delivered to the company for review.
